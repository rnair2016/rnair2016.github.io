Hardware Identification Chart

Motherboard A motherboard holds all of the parts of a computer together. It holds together many of the necessary components of a computer.
Hard Disk Drive A hard drive is a permanent data storage device in the computer. It stores data on its disks, that is why it is also known as hard disk drives. It is used for both storing and retrieving data.
Graphics Card A graphics card controls and enhances what is shown on the display screen of your computer.
Power supply A power supply unit converts the alternate current to direct current for all of the parts inside a computer
Audio Card An audio card is a device that attaches to the PCI slot on motherboard to let the computer deliver sound.
